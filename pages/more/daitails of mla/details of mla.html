GUMMIDIPOONDI ASSEMBLY ELECTION RESULTS 2021

Gummidipoondi is an assembly constituency in Tamil Nadu. In 2021 Tamil Nadu Assembly elections, the total percentage of voters in Gummidipoondi Assembly Constituency was recorded at 77.93 percent.Gummidipoondi comes under Tiruvallur district of Tamil Nadu State. T J Govindarajan (DMK), Prakash (PMK), V.Saravanan (IJK), Usha (NTK), K.M. Dilli (DMDK) are key candidates contested from Gummidipoondi constituency in the 2021 Tamil Nadu Assembly elections along with many other independent candidates. In 2021, T J Govindarajan of the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam won the seat by defeating Prakash of the Pattali Makkal Katchi with a margin of 50938 votes.
